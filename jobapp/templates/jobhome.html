  WHAT IS JOBSEEKER


Job seeker refers to an individual who is actively looking for an employment opportunity/job and has submitted an application to the employer.

Types of job seekers

  1. Those who seek: actively searching for a job
  2. Those who monitor: not actively searching but keeping their eye on roles and opportunities
  3. Those who are settled: not doing anything to look for a new job
